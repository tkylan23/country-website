Food

Explore the diverse and delicious cuisine of Sri Lanka, from traditional dishes to mouthwatering street food.

Rolls

Delicious Mutton Rolls are a popular Sri Lankan snack. Crispy pastry wraps encase a flavorful filling of spiced minced mutton, creating a savory and satisfying treat..

Kottu Roti

Kottu Roti is a Sri Lankan street food delight. Chopped flatbread mixed with a medley of vegetables, eggs, and your choice of meat, all seasoned with aromatic spices, making for a hearty and tasty dish..

String Hoppers

String Hoppers are a traditional Sri Lankan dish made from steamed rice noodle nests. These delicate, fine strands of rice noodles are often served with curries, providing a light and delightful culinary experience..
